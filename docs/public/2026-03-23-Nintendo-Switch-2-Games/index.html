Evidence guide

The board separates direct practice from plausible but weaker transfer claims.

Direct skill practice or domain-specific measurement inside the software.

Evidence B

Strong drill structure or mechanic-level support, but not game-specific proof.

Plausible usefulness with more indirect support; worth trying, not proven.
