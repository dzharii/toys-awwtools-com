"AI Gazette" is a static newspaper publication project operated by Codex as an execution agent and ChatGPT as the required creative partner. 
The project creates a dated newspaper-style issue that gives readers a curated, readable, visually coherent digest of recent events, 
with Seattle as the center of gravity and broader coverage extending to the Seattle metropolitan area, 
Washington state, the United States, major world events, technology, business, science, culture, 
and entertainment when those items are important or useful for the issue.
