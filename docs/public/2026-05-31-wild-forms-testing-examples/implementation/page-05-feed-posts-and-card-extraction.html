Forum Question And Answer Thread

This example imitates a technical forum question page. It tests extraction of the question title, author, score, tags, accepted answer, answer authors, answer scores, nested comments, and timestamps while preserving hierarchy.

How should I extract values from a custom dropdown rendered outside the form?

Score 372,184 views3 answersasked May 30, 2026, 4:18 PM by dom_mapper

I am building a browser tool that needs to capture the visible selected value from custom dropdowns. Some menus are rendered in a portal near the end of the body, while the label is inside the original form. What signals should I use to preserve the relationship?

htmlariaformsbrowser-automation

3 Answers

AcceptedScore 52answered May 30, 2026, 5:02 PM by layout_sleuth

Start with the accessible name and active descendant relationship. Then look for hidden backing inputs, aria-controls, aria-labelledby, and nearby form context. If the menu is portaled, preserve both the trigger location and the menu item state.

pixelroot · yesterday: The hidden input point is the part most people miss.
dom_mapper · yesterday: Thanks, this helped me connect the trigger and option list.
Score 18answered May 31, 2026, 8:44 AM by testwright

In practice I also inspect visible text order. ARIA is ideal, but many production widgets have incomplete relationships.

Score 9answered May 31, 2026, 10:12 AM by semanticist

If the dropdown is searchable, capture the current selected chip separately from the option list. The filtered list may not contain the selected value at the moment of extraction.
